My Journey.

Hi, I'm Vishal, an AI Engineer specializing in Computer Vision.

My journey into technology didn't start in a classroom; it began as a hobby. Coming from a non-IT background, my fascination with game development was my self-driven entry into the world of code. It was this hobby that ignited my passion and led me to pursue a degree in Computer Science.

The pivotal moment came when I started working on Augmented Reality. I realized my true passion wasn't just creating virtual worlds, but in teaching computers to see and understand our world. This discovery shifted my focus entirely to Computer Vision

Today, I channel that same passion into building end-to-end AI solutions. I architect and train deep learning models for tasks like object detection and facial recognition and deploy them in practical, full-stack applications. I'm driven by the challenge of turning complex visual data into meaningful, real-world impact.
